MULTIPLIER GUIDELINES (for rescaling):
Use multipliers relative to current size, NOT absolute sizes.

Visibility problems:
- Invisible/tiny specks: suggestedMultiplier 3-10 (make much larger)
- Barely visible: suggestedMultiplier 2-4 (make larger)

Proportion problems:
- Slightly too small: suggestedMultiplier 1.5-2.5
- Slightly too large: suggestedMultiplier 0.5-0.7
- Way too large/dominating: suggestedMultiplier 0.1-0.4

Examples:
- "tombstones are invisible specks" → suggestedMultiplier: 5
- "flower patch larger than house" → suggestedMultiplier: 0.25
- "trees slightly overshadow buildings" → suggestedMultiplier: 0.6

REFINEMENT GUIDELINES
- Use rescaleAssets FIRST to fix size problems (this is often the best fix!)
- Use suggestedMultiplier (relative) rather than absolute sizes
- Use removeAssets to eliminate clutter or duplicates
- Adding assets is good for filling empty areas
- Move assets only if they're clearly in the wrong spot
- Terrain changes should support asset placement
- Keep the refinement focused (1-5 changes max)
